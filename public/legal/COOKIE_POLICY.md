# Cookie Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

## 1. What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files stored on your device to remember preferences and improve user experience.

## 2. Cookie Types

Essential Cookies (No Consent Required)
- Authentication: session management
- Security: CSRF tokens, rate limiting
- Functionality: language, theme preferences
- Duration: session or 1 year

Analytics Cookies (Consent Required)
- Google Analytics: user behavior, traffic sources
- Vercel Analytics: performance metrics
- Resend: email engagement tracking
- Duration: 2 years

Marketing Cookies (Consent Required)
- Third-party tracking pixels
- Retargeting and conversion tracking
- Social media integrations
- Duration: 6 months to 2 years

## 3. Consent Management
- Consent banner on first visit
- Withdrawal available in Settings
- Some services allow granular consent

## 4. Third-Party Cookies
External services may set cookies:
- Stripe (payments)
- Google (analytics)
- Social platforms (login)
See their privacy policies for details.

## 5. Cookie Blocking
You can disable cookies via browser settings.
Note: essential functionality may be impaired.

## 6. Your Rights
GDPR Article 82 covers cookie rights.
