# GDPR Information Notice

For Shop Operators and Customers

## Data Controller & Processor
- Controller: Merchant (shop owner) for customer data
- Processor: Fidelio S.r.l. for platform operations

## Data Collection
We collect:
- Identification: name, email, phone
- Location: address, coordinates
- Transaction: purchases, points, rewards
- Device: IP address, browser data
- Staff: employee information if multi-user

## How We Use Your Data
- Operate loyalty programs
- Process transactions and rewards
- Send transactional communications
- Fraud prevention and security
- Tax and legal compliance
- Platform analytics (anonymized)

## Responsibilities
Shop operators must:
- Inform customers about data collection
- Obtain consent for marketing
- Establish lawful processing basis
- Respond to privacy rights requests
- Report data breaches
- Maintain retention policies

## Your Rights
You can:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request erasure
- Request data portability
- Withdraw consent
- Lodge complaints to supervisory authorities

## Data Safeguards
- TLS encryption in transit
- AES-256 encryption at rest
- Access controls by role
- Regular security audits
- Incident response plan
- Staff confidentiality agreements

## Retention
- Customer data: while active plus 2 years
- Transactions: 7 years (tax requirement)
- Support tickets: 3 years
- Audit logs: 1 year
