# Data Processing Agreement

Fidelio S.r.l. - Processor Services

Effective: April 2026

## Parties
- Controller: Merchant/Shop Owner
- Processor: Fidelio S.r.l.

## Processing Scope
Fidelio processes customer data for loyalty program operation during subscription term.

## Data Subject
End customers of the merchant, including:
- Personal identification data
- Transaction and purchase records
- Behavioral and engagement data
- Device and location data

## Processor Obligations
Fidelio shall:
- Process data only per Controller instructions
- Ensure staff confidentiality
- Implement appropriate security (Article 32)
- Assist with DPIA (Data Protection Impact Assessment)
- Notify of data breaches within 72 hours
- Respond to data subject rights requests
- Delete data upon termination
- Maintain processing records
- Undergo annual security audits

## Sub-Processors
Authorized third-party processors:
- Stripe (payment processing)
- Vercel (hosting and CDN)
- Clerk (authentication)
- SendGrid/Resend (email delivery)
- Google (analytics)

30-day notice provided for changes.

## Data Subject Rights
Processor assists with requests for:
- Right of access (Article 15)
- Right to rectification (Article 16)
- Right to erasure (Article 17)
- Right to restrict processing (Article 18)
- Right to data portability (Article 20)
- Right to object (Article 21)

## Security Measures
- Encryption: TLS in transit, AES-256 at rest
- Authentication: multi-factor where applicable
- Access controls: least privilege principle
- Network security: firewalls, intrusion detection
- Staff training: GDPR and security protocols
- Incident response: 24-hour escalation
- Data backup: daily backups with recovery tested
- Business continuity: RTO less than 4 hours

## Term & Termination
- Term: Duration of processor relationship
- Termination: Upon contract end
- Data handling: Return or deletion within 30 days

## Liability
Processor liable for GDPR compliance violations.
Standard limitation of liability clause applies.
Professional liability insurance maintained.

## Audit Rights
Controller may:
- Request documentation of processing
- Conduct security audits
- Require SOC 2 or ISO 27001 certification
- Engage independent third-party auditors
- Inspect facilities (with notice)

## International Transfers
Data remains within EU/EEA.
Any necessary transfers use SCCs (standard contractual clauses).

## Standard Contractual Clauses
For transfers outside EU, SCCs per EU Decision 2021/914.

## Governing Law
This DPA is governed by GDPR (EU) 2016/679.
